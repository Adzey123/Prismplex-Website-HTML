Ranks Support the Server

Unfortunately, Minecraft servers are not free to run. There are monthly upkeep costs to keep the server going, plus various other expenses such as our occasional purchase or commission of a plugin or paying to keep our prismplex domain. As the community grows and there are more concurrent players online, we might also need to purchase additional gigabytes of server space to meet the increasing needs of server memory.

To make sure we can keep Prismplex active and growing, we allow members of our community to help support our server costs financially in exchange for Premium Ranks that come with cool perks and cosmetics.  Buying a rank is never expected or necessary to play on the Prismplex. Rather, this system allows those who are able and willing to support the server financially to do so while getting fun perks in exchange!

We will always strive to ensure our server is not pay to play or pay to win, so the perks that come with premium ranks are mostly cosmetic, as well as some quality of life advantages. For example, the various town mines on Azure SMP allow members with premium ranks to gather certain minerals faster than they otherwise would by mining for them in the overworld.

Moreover, unlike most other servers, instead of being a one-time purchase, our ranks are paid for on a monthly basis. This allows us to keep the overall rank costs lower than most other Minecraft servers and lets players cancel their rank at any time if they are no longer happy with it or are no longer as active on the Prismplex. We also believe in this model because we are confident in our ability to continuously provide a fun and enjoyable gameplay experience for our players, and if they ever feel like they no longer enjoy the server then they can choose not to renew their rank rather than feeling stuck if they had paid much more for a permanent rank up front.
